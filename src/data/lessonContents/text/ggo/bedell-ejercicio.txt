# Practical worksheet overview
- Download the official template **“Calcular la importancia del sistema de información – Método Bedell”** and keep the column labels in Spanish while annotating English hints next to each criterion.
- Clarify the problem statement in English, then restate the business objective (**objetivo de negocio**) in Spanish to mirror the slide deck phrasing.

## Step 1 · Define evaluation axes (ejes de evaluación)
- Confirm that the three axes — *Business Impact (Impacto en el negocio)*, *Organisational Impact (Impacto en la organización)*, and *IS/IT Impact (Impacto en los SI/TI)* — align with the stakeholders you will interview.
- Use a short English note to explain how each axis links to the corporate strategy, keeping the Spanish header untouched.

## Step 2 · Score impact (valorar impacto)
- For each requirement, choose a score from 0 to 5 and justify it with a bilingual sentence, e.g. “High regulatory exposure (**alta exposición regulatoria**) justifies a 5 because the system automates reporting.”
- Check that the numeric justification reflects the template’s guidelines on intensity (**intensidad**) and pervasiveness (**alcance**).

## Step 3 · Capture reason (motivo)
- In the *Reason (Motivo)* column, write a concise explanation in English followed by the Spanish keyword that appears in the PDF, such as “Improves cycle time (**mejora el tiempo de ciclo**)”.
- Highlight assumptions about budget or technology constraints so they can be validated during the steering committee review (**comité de dirección**).

## Step 4 · Summarise totals (resumen de totales)
- Sum the impact scores for each axis and verify that the totals match the Bedell matrix (**matriz Bedell**).
- Provide an English executive summary that quotes the Spanish axis names to maintain traceability when sharing the worksheet.

## Deliverable checklist
- Completed spreadsheet with bilingual justifications per row.
- One-paragraph recommendation explaining how the highest score supports alignment (**alineación negocio–TI**).
- List of open questions for stakeholders who need follow-up, naming each role in Spanish with an English descriptor in parentheses.
