# Topic 3 · Business and IS/IT Alignment (Alineación entre negocio y SI/TI)

## Objectives (Objetivos)
- Understand why business and IT must share a strategic vision (visión estratégica).
- Analyze the Strategic Alignment Model (Modelo de Alineamiento Estratégico) from Henderson and Venkatraman.
- Review tools for building coherent strategies in both domains.

## Key concepts (Conceptos clave)
- Organizations develop **business strategy (estrategia de negocio)** and **information systems/IT strategy (estrategia de SI/TI)** at the same time, and each materializes through organizational and technological infrastructures.
- Alignment aims to ensure changes in one dimension are reflected in the other in a coordinated way.
- The absence of IT strategic planning (planificación estratégica de TI) raises risk and limits the value delivered.

## Strategic Alignment Model (Modelo de Alineamiento Estratégico, SAM)
- Proposes four perspectives:
  1. **Strategy execution (Ejecución estratégica):** business strategy guides how IT is organized.
  2. **Technology transformation (Transformación tecnológica):** IT strategy drives new business capabilities.
  3. **Competitive potential (Potencial competitivo):** IT enables differentiated advantages.
  4. **Service level (Nivel de servicio):** the IT infrastructure supports internal processes.
- Distinguishes two axes: **strategic fit (ajuste estratégico)**—external vs. internal—and **functional integration (integración funcional)**—business vs. IT.

## Tools for defining strategy (Herramientas para definir estrategia)
- **SWOT analysis (Análisis DAFO):** identifies strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
- **PESTEL analysis (Análisis PESTEL):** reviews political, economic, social, technological, environmental, and legal factors.
- **Porter’s five forces (Cinco fuerzas de Porter):** evaluates competitors, customers, suppliers, new entrants, and substitutes.
- Apply these tools to both business and IS/IT to maintain coherence.

## Strategic management process (Proceso de dirección estratégica)
1. Analyze the environment and stakeholders (entorno y stakeholders) to detect opportunities and risks.
2. Assess internal resources (recursos internos) to recognize strengths and weaknesses.
3. Define market-oriented mission and vision (misión y visión orientadas al mercado).
4. Formulate and implement strategies (formular e implementar estrategias).
5. Evaluate results and adjust as needed (evaluar resultados y ajustar).

## Alignment good practices (Buenas prácticas de alineación)
- Include IT leaders (responsables de TI) when defining corporate objectives.
- Translate business priorities into technology roadmaps (hojas de ruta tecnológicas).
- Review the project portfolio regularly to guarantee strategic contribution.
- Establish shared indicators between business and IT (indicadores compartidos entre negocio y TI).
